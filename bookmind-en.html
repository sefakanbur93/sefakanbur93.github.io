Privacy Policy

Protecting your personal data is important to us. The app BookMind was developed to run entirely offline. All data – except for advertising-related information – remains solely on your device and is not transmitted to us or any third parties.

1. Offline Usage

The app can be used completely offline. No personal data such as name, email address, content, or usage behavior is collected, stored, or transmitted.

2. Advertising

In order to provide the app for free, ads from third-party providers are displayed. Personalized advertising may be shown based on your interests and app interactions.

Advertising networks may process the following data:

  • Device information (e.g. model, OS version, language)
  • Advertising ID (e.g. Google Advertising ID, Apple IDFA)
  • Approximate location data
  • App interaction and ad engagement data

This data is processed exclusively by the advertising providers. Erdmann Kanbur GbR does not access or store any of this data.

3. Consent and Withdrawal

Upon first launch of the app, users are asked for consent to show personalized ads. This consent is voluntary and can be withdrawn at any time in the app settings.
