Privacy Policy

Protecting your personal data is important to us. The app BookMind is designed to work completely offline. All usage data – with the exception of advertising – remains on your device and is not transmitted to us or third parties.

1. Offline Usage

The app can be used entirely offline. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data such as names, email addresses, content, or usage behavior.

2. Advertising

To support free access to the app, advertisements from third-party providers are displayed. Personalized ads may be shown based on your interests and interaction.

Advertising providers may process the following data:

  • Device information (e.g. model, OS version, language)
  • Advertising ID (e.g. Google Advertising ID or Apple IDFA)
  • Approximate location
  • Interaction with ads within the app

This data is processed solely by the ad networks. Erdmann Kanbur GbR does not access or store any of this information.

3. Consent and Withdrawal

Upon first launch of the app, you are asked to consent to the use of personalized advertising. This consent is optional and can be withdrawn at any time via the app settings.
